Before the Recordable

A near-miss reporting and analytics concept designed to reveal safety risk earlier.

RoleCapstone research, solution design, and presentation
ToolsQR workflow, dashboard concept, leading indicators, pilot planning, technical presentation
FocusProcess Improvement • Safety • Analytics

Challenge

Find a practical way for a contractor to identify repeated hazards before they become recordable incidents.

Approach

Designed a quick QR-based reporting flow: scan, report, analyze, and act. Proposed a 30-to-60-day pilot that tracks reporting time, repeated hazards, response time, corrective actions, and recurrence after action.

Outcome

Created a realistic implementation concept that turns individual near misses into measurable leading indicators for project leadership and safety teams.

What I Learned

  • Reporting has to be fast enough for field participation.
  • A dashboard is useful only when it drives visible corrective action.
  • Trust and a learning-focused culture are critical to collecting honest near-miss information.
