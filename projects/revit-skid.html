Parametric Skid and Revit Coordination

Turning 2D project information into coordinated, buildable model content.

RoleCollege Project Engineer Intern, Virtual Design and Construction
ToolsAutodesk Revit, AutoCAD, Bluebeam Revu, Autodesk Construction Cloud, Evolve
FocusCad • Vdc • Construction Engineering

Challenge

Translate project drawings and design information into accurate Revit content that could support coordination, shop drawings, installation planning, and modular fabrication.

Approach

Built and revised model content from AutoCAD files, project prints, and existing plan information. Supported configurable Revit family development for skid and mounting components while checking dimensions, installation constraints, and constructability.

Outcome

Created clearer, repeatable model information for design and field teams while helping identify coordination questions before installation.

What I Learned

  • Parametric families are most valuable when their controls match real fabrication and installation decisions.
  • Model accuracy must be paired with drawing review and field awareness.
  • Good VDC work reduces ambiguity between design intent and physical construction.
EnvironmentLarge-scale data centers
OutputModel + shop drawing support
