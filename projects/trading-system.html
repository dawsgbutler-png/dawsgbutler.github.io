AI Trading Decision System

A modular paper-trading platform for scanning, scoring, risk control, and performance review.

RoleIndependent system designer and developer
ToolsPython, Alpaca API, automated logging, backtesting, replay testing, risk controls
FocusPython • Automation • Data Systems

Challenge

Create a system that could evaluate a broad stock universe, separate stronger opportunities from weak setups, execute paper trades, and record enough information to improve the decision process.

Approach

Developed modular scanning, scoring, order-management, error-recovery, and reporting logic. Added position limits, stale-order handling, end-of-day controls, cooldowns, and replay/backtest workflows. The interface shown is a concept visualization for the system's Mission Control dashboard.

Outcome

Built a working paper-trading research environment that connects software architecture, data logic, execution safeguards, and performance analysis.

What I Learned

  • A decision system is only as useful as its logging, failure handling, and ability to explain why an action occurred.
  • Backtesting must include realistic assumptions such as slippage, position limits, and unavailable fills.
  • Risk controls and system reliability are engineering requirements, not optional features.
EnvironmentPaper trading only
